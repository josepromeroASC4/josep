About Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is one of the best players the game of soccer has ever seen. He plays for FC Barcelona and for the Argentinian National Team. He wears the number 10 and plays the position of forward.

Timeline

Birth

Lionel Messi was born in Rosario, Argentina on June 24th, 1987. He was born to working class parents and has two brothers and one sister.

La Masia

At the age of 13, Messi was offered a place at FC Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia. He moved to Barcelona at the age of 13.

Professional Debut

Messi made his professional debut for FC Barcelona on October 16th, 2004.

Balon D'or

Messi won his first Balon D'or trophy in 2009.

Champions League 2015

Messi won the Champions League with FC Barcelona in 2015

500th goal

Messi scored his 500th goal agaisnt Real Madrid on April 23rd, 2017.
